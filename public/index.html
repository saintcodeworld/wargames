HOW IT WORKS
GETTING STARTED
1. Sign up to create a new account. A unique keypair (public key + private key) is generated for you.
2. Save your private key somewhere safe. It is the only way to log back into your account.
3. Deposit SOL to your account balance using the DEPOSIT button in the lobby.
CREATING / JOINING A MATCH
1. Select a game mode: 1v1 (2 players) or 2v2 (4 players).
2. Choose FREE PLAY to practice with no entry fee, or pick a SOL entry fee (0.01 / 0.05 / 0.1 / 0.5 SOL) to play for real.
3. Click CREATE FREE ROOM or CREATE ROOM, or join an existing room from the ACTIVE BATTLEFIELDS list.
4. Once all player slots are filled, a 3-second countdown starts and the match begins.
GAMEPLAY
W / Up Arrow -- Move up / Jump
S / Down Arrow -- Crouch (in trenches)
A / Left Arrow -- Move left
D / Right Arrow -- Move right
SPACE or F -- Shoot
You are assigned to either USA or IRAN. Eliminate opponents to score kills for your team.
ROUNDS AND WINNING
Matches are best-of-3 rounds. Each round has a kill target.
The first team to reach the kill target wins the round.
Win 2 out of 3 rounds to win the match.
PRIZE POOL AND PAYOUTS
All entry fees are combined into a prize pool (minus a small platform fee).
The winning team splits the prize pool evenly among its members.
Winnings are credited to your in-game balance automatically.
LEADERBOARD
The leaderboard tracks wins, losses, kills, and deaths over the last 24 hours.
The top-ranked player earns a 2 SOL reward.
